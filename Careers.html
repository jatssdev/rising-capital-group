Empowering employee networks for collective success

  • Women of Rising Community

    Rising Capital Group's Women Empowerment Networks, including Women in Finance and Women in Leadership, are dedicated to supporting and connecting women within our firm. These networks provide opportunities for professional development, mentorship, and meaningful collaboration to help women thrive and succeed in their careers.

  • Rising Pride

    Rising Unity fosters an inclusive and supportive environment for all team members, promoting diversity, raising awareness, and actively contributing to the community.

  • Rising’s Family Community

    The Rising Family Network supports employees at every stage of caregiving, fostering a culture that values balance between professional responsibilities and family life.

  • Rising’s Multicultural Community

    The Rising Capital Group Multicultural Network fosters inclusivity by promoting awareness and understanding of race, culture, and ethnicity, creating a supportive environment where all employees can thrive.

  • Rising Volunteer Community

    The Rising Capital Group Volunteer Network offers employees the opportunity to collaborate while making a positive impact on the community through meaningful volunteer initiatives.

  • Inclusion Network

    The Inclusion Network drives the creation of a more inclusive workplace by hosting events, facilitating discussions, inviting guest speakers, and providing networking opportunities.
